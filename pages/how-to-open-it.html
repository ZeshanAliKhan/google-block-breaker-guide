How to open Google Block Breaker in Search

If Google is surfacing the game for your setup, the launch path is simple. The important part is starting in Search rather than drifting into unrelated app results.

  1. Open Google Search on desktop or mobile.
  2. Search for Block Breaker.
  3. If you do not see the playable card, try Brick Breaker.
  4. Look for the game panel with a Play button.
  5. Launch the game directly from Search.
Useful distinction:

If you are seeing Play Store listings instead of a Search game panel, you are looking at separate third-party apps, not Google’s built-in Search version.

If the game card still does not show up, try a different device, another browser session, or the Google app on mobile. Availability can vary by session, platform, region, and how Google is surfacing the feature at that moment.
